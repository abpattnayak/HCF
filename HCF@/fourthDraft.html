Our Story

Human capital foundation (HCF) is a boutique Management consulting Firm focussed on building cutting edge management practices for Enterprises & Organizations. We are centred on themes of people, performance and culture confronting Boards, leaders and Teams in diverse operative contexts.


Our core strength is the ability to understand your organizational reality and bring right perspectives to build and execute effective solutions. Our insights come from the world of business, non-profits, academics and start-ups. Our idea, tools and approaches build around HR, Psychology, Systems thinking & OD. We pride ourselves in putting us in your shoes, ask insightful and honest questions and provide elegant and practical outcome.

Our Core Practices

Productivity & Performance Practices

One of the fundamental themes in the context of business landscape today; is the challenge of Organizational and Personal accountability and creation of measurable impact. Our practioners bring both internal Organizational Performance and External stakeholder perspectives to help you design robust performance tools, processes and metrics to help your Organization create the difference through Impact

Human Context Practice

We believe organizations thrive when Individuals lead purposeful and fulfilled lives in their Personal and professional sphere. Through interactive, therapeutic and facilitative intervention our team of trained counsellors help people find meaning in what and how they do. As part of the intervention and engagement individuals not only attempt to recognise their personal challenges, limitations, behaviour patterns but also commit themselves for total wellbeing.

Employee Engagement Practice

Engaged employees make a positive difference to their Organization. Effective engagement is at the heart of productive, happy and thriving workplaces. Engaged employees stay committed to their Firms, play the role of brand ambassadors for their companies and always strive to take their companies to the next stage. We offer research based engagement tools, diagnostics and onsite interventions to ensure that leadership always has the pulse of the Workforce.

Leadership & Organization Effectiveness Practice

Leadership & Organizational Capacity Building is a vital challenge & Opportunity for organizations worldwide. We look at ways to help you build and sustain effective leadership and Organizational capacity through a combination of interventions, Training, process work, Onsite and offsite coaching & mentoring approaches.
